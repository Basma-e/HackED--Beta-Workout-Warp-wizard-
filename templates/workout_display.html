Some tips and tricks:

  • Always remeber to warm up properly. This could be some light cardio and dynamic streches.
  • Focus on maintaining proper form during exercises to prevent injuries and maximize effectiveness.
  • If your goal is to increase muscle growth the best rest period between sets is 30-90 seconds.
  • If your goal is to increase muscle endurance the best rest period between sets is 30 seconds or less.
  • If you complete a set with little difficulty increase the weight you are using and vice versa.
  • Pay attention to how your body feels during the workout. If something doesn't feel right, modify or skip the exercise.
  • Finish your workout with a cool-down, including static stretches, to improve flexibility and reduce muscle soreness.
  • Most importantly, have fun!
